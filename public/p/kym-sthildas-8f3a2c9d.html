Good to catch up, Kym!

This is a package I’ve put together off the back of our chat.

What I’m hearing is that your aim is to upskill in agentic workflows, so you can produce and ship more of the work yourself, faster, while keeping the output consistent (and on brand).

The key to this is a memory system, so any AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini) can read your whole context.

I’ve put together a few packages. Each one’s built with you, so you own it and understand how it works, and I’ll leave you with a whole bunch of tried and tested workflow skills and templates to get your content pipelines humming!
